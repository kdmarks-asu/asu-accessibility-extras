Form Labels

Before we delve into the next part of our discussion, please fill out the following form. Be sure to include all the required information.



Are you having trouble filling in the form and knowing which checkbox or button to select? 

Users of assistive technology face unclear forms like this every day. That's why it's so important to use labels correctly on all input fields.

In addition, don't rely on placeholder text or visual cues such as colors or icons to convey an input field's purpose (not everyone can see them clearly). Finally, indicating which fields are required and what formats are allowed is helpful for everyone.
