The Law & Standards

As dry as they may seem, it's important to know the regulations and standards that apply to ASU's web sites, applications, and content. Laws such as the Rehabilitation Act of 1973 and the Americans with Disability Act were created to protect the rights of disabled persons and are being applied to all types of electronic information technology.

For internet accessibility, the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) is the standard cited by the Department of Justice for their web accessibility rulings and the standard ASU complies with for all its digital content.
