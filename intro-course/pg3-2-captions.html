Many Students Use Captions

In a 2016 study by Oregon State and 3PlayMedia of 2,124 college and university students, 34.9% of participants said they “always” or “often” use closed captions. Of participants without hearing difficulties, 71% use captions at least some of the time. The top reasons given by students for using captions were to help them focus and retain information.

At ASU, all online video must be captioned.
