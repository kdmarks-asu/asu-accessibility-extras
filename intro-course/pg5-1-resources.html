University Resources

Arizona State University is home to numerous groups who provide academic and technical support, advocacy, and services for people with disabilities.

Disability Resource Center

ASU Disability Resource Center (DRC) facilitates academic accommodations and services for students with disabilities to ensure everyone has equal access to course materials and activities. DRC offices are located at the Downtown, Polytechnic, Tempe, and West locations. You can contact any DRC office:

  • Phone: 480-965-1234
  • FAX: 480-965-0441
  • Email: DRC@asu.edu

UTO Web Accessibility

ASU's UTO Web Accessibility Team holds regular office hours, as well as free trainings and consultations for web content creators, development teams, and others on meeting ASU web accessibility requirements. They also maintain the ASU Web Accessibility help documentation. As part of the UTO's UX Center of Excellence, the team offers fee-based website and application audits and VPAT verifications.

  • Email: webaccessibility@asu.edu

ASU Libraries Disability Services

ASU Libraries offers a variety of services and assistive technology for users with disabilities on all ASU campuses. See a full list of locations.

  • Phone: 480-965-0573 (TTY users, please dial 711)
  • Email: Jamie.Schendel@asu.edu

Office of Equity and Inclusion

The Office of Equity and Inclusion facilitates ASU's ADA interactive process and assists in matters pertaining to compliance with ASU policy.
